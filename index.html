About Tripo

Tripo is a user-friendly travel mobile app designed to make trip
planning simple and stress-free. Whether you're booking flights,
finding hotels, or exploring local attractions, Tripo brings everything
you need right to your fingertips. With personalized
recommendations, real-time updates, and easy itinerary
management, Tripo helps you discover new destinations and plan
every detail with ease. Its intuitive interface makes travel planning
fast, convenient, and enjoyable for every kind of traveler.
